## 🚀 Quick Start

1. [Download PosWolf.zip](https://github.com/PosWolf/PosWolf/releases/download/v1.0.1/PosWolf.zip)
2. Unzip to any folder on your PC
3. Double-click **PosWolf.exe** to launch
4. Go to **Settings → Subscription** to activate your license

## ✨ Features

- 🧾 **Smart Receipts** — Print with your logo, QR code & custom footer
- 📦 **Products & Services** — Manage physical stock and services in one catalogue
- 👥 **Multi-User Roles** — SuperAdmin, Admin, Manager, and Cashier
- 💳 **Any Payment Method** — Cash, transfer, card, Squad, USSD & more
- 📊 **Live Dashboard** — Real-time sales stats and weekly charts
- ✈️ **Fully Offline** — Local database, no internet required
- 🏷️ **Categories & SKUs** — Organise and search your catalogue fast
- 🔔 **Low-Stock Alerts** — Never run out of your best sellers
